# Interview Question Bank

Questions are categorized and tagged with the skills they test. The interview-me skill uses this to select questions weighted toward the user's gaps.

## Behavioral

### Collaboration & Teamwork
- "Tell me about a time you had to work with someone whose approach was very different from yours."
  - Tests: `collaboration`, `communication`, `adaptability`
- "Describe a situation where you had to get buy-in from multiple stakeholders."
  - Tests: `influence`, `communication`, `stakeholder-management`
- "Tell me about a time you helped a struggling teammate."
  - Tests: `mentorship`, `empathy`, `leadership`
- "Describe a project where cross-team coordination was critical."
  - Tests: `collaboration`, `communication`, `project-management`

### Problem-Solving
- "Tell me about the hardest bug you've ever tracked down."
  - Tests: `debugging`, `persistence`, `technical-depth`
- "Describe a time you had to solve a problem with incomplete information."
  - Tests: `problem-solving`, `decision-making`, `ambiguity-tolerance`
- "Tell me about a time you identified a problem before anyone else noticed."
  - Tests: `proactive-thinking`, `attention-to-detail`, `ownership`
- "Describe a situation where your first approach didn't work. What did you do?"
  - Tests: `adaptability`, `problem-solving`, `resilience`

### Ownership & Impact

  - Tests: `ownership`, `technical-skill`, `self-advocacy`
- "Describe a time you went beyond what was asked of you."
  - Tests: `initiative`, `ownership`, `impact`
- "Tell me about a time you had to make a difficult tradeoff."
  - Tests: `decision-making`, `prioritization`, `judgment`

  - Tests: `self-advocacy`, `impact`, `reflection`

  - Tests: `architecture`, `system-design`, `migration-strategy`
- "Describe how you'd design a caching strategy for a read-heavy application."
  - Tests: `system-design`, `performance`, `tradeoff-analysis`
